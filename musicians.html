Musicians

We are a group of musicians who are passionate about chamber music and love to make music together.

Ellyn Liu

Violin

Ellyn is a classically trained violinist with a BA in Music from the University of Chicago. She has played with the Seattle Philharmonic Orchestra and the Seattle Metropolitan Chamber Orchestra, and has years of experience playing live music for weddings and parties. She is particularly fond of chamber music and enjoys performing in an intimate atmosphere.

Raymond Hsu

Viola

A Seattle-native, Raymond is a violist with a BA in Music with a Concentration in Viola Performance from Dartmouth College. He has soloed with the Dartmouth Symphony Orchestra and plays in the Seattle Philharmonic Orchestra. Raymond loves performing chamber music and is ecstatic to share his love of music with everyone.

Ken Lin

Violin

Ken is a seasoned violinist hailing from the Issaquah area. A longtime pupil of the Marcus Talley studio, Ken has soloed in concert with the Evergreen Philharmonic twice and served as concertmaster for their 2017 season, before studying with Ron Patternson at the University of Washington and playing with the UW Symphony. Ken now proudly plays with the Seattle Philharmonic and holds a wealth of chamber music experience.

Sean-Wyn Ng

Cello

Sean-Wyn is a cellist from Mesa, Arizona. She has soloed with the Princeton Sinfonia and has also served as principal cellist of the Phoenix Youth Symphony. She currently plays with the Seattle Philharmonic orchestra and is excited to perform chamber works with other musicians in the Seattle area.
