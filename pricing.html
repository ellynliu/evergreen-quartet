Pricing

Musicians typically arrive 30 minutes prior to the event to set up. Unless otherwise requested, musicians dress in concert black.

String Quartet*

Violin I, Violin II, Viola, Cello

  • 1 hour: $400, 2 hours: $600

String Trio

Violin, Viola, Cello

  • 1 hour: $350, 2 hours: $525

String Duo

Two Violins; Violin and Viola; or Violin and Cello

  • 1 hour: $300, 2 hours: $450

Solo

Violin, Viola, or Cello

  • 1 hour: $250, 2 hours: $375

Other fees

Booking and consultation

  • A $40 booking fee secures the date and time of your event.
  • This fee covers the cost of one of our quartet members to work with you with song selections and provide other consultation.

Travel

  • Musicians will travel up to 40 miles free of charge (measured from Downtown Seattle).
*For the best musical experience, we recommend a string quartet for larger events such as weddings and parties.
